03 : About me

Kingsley David is a Chennai, based front-end engineer who creates well-designed websites and e-commerce stores. A data nerd at heart, Kingsley majored in Computer Application (BCA) at ALM College India, and has used his data-analysis powers mainly for good. Like many of his generation, he was drawn to coding and design originally as an angsty teen trying to make his MySpace page look cool–but not too cool.

Since those days he has been pretty busy both in-house and as a freelancer, and with his YouTube channel where he teaches people how to use Sketch. He has a variety of experience such as creating custom Shopify themes, UI development in React, and speaks fluent HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Liquid. (note from editor: one could also add sarcasm to that list).

Kingsley’s loves to tackle a challenge head-on by analyzing it and coming up with a plan to solve the needs of his clients that makes sense for their big-picture goals. Most recently, he’s working as a Senior Software Engineer at HCL, and has previously as a Software Engineer at Virtusa in sunny Chennai, and previously, he was a Front End Developer at The Deal, Chennai India. He’s also taking on select freelance projects.
